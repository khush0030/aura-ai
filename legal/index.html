Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Aura AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Aura AI iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth)
  • Tone profile: Your communication style preferences from the onboarding quiz
  • Usage data: Feature usage counts for enforcing daily limits (no content is stored)
  • Analytics: Anonymised usage events via PostHog (feature taps, session data). No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Crash reports: Error logs via Sentry to help us fix bugs. No personal content is included.

2. What We Do NOT Store

  • Screenshots you upload — processed in real-time and immediately discarded
  • Dating profile bios you paste — processed and discarded
  • Conversation threads you share — processed and discarded
  • AI-generated messages — never saved to our servers

All AI processing happens through our secure backend (Supabase Edge Functions). Your content is never stored or used for training.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the Aura AI service
  • To enforce daily usage limits on the free tier
  • To improve the app based on anonymised usage patterns
  • To send transactional emails (password reset, account confirmation)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — database and authentication provider
  • Anthropic — AI processing (your content is processed but not stored per Anthropic's API terms)
  • OpenAI — screenshot text extraction (processed, not stored)
  • PostHog — anonymised analytics
  • Sentry — crash reporting

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained until you delete your account. Usage logs are automatically deleted after 90 days. You can request full data deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data: Contact us at privacy@aura-ai.app
  • Delete your account and all associated data: Settings → Profile → Delete Account (coming in v1.1) or email us
  • Opt out of analytics: Contact us at privacy@aura-ai.app

7. Children's Privacy

Aura AI is rated 17+ and is not intended for users under 17. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 17.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy: privacy@aura-ai.app
