## Description (4000 chars max)

Aura AI is your personal dating conversation coach — built to help you send messages that actually sound like you, on your best day.

Most AI dating tools generate the same generic openers for everyone. Aura AI is different. It learns your communication style in a 5-minute quiz, then uses that profile to generate every opener, reply, and bio rewrite so it sounds like you wrote it — not a robot.

**Four features. One goal: better conversations.**

💬 Chat Reply
Upload a screenshot of any dating app conversation — Tinder, Hinge, Bumble, Instagram, iMessage. Aura AI reads the full context and generates 3 personalised replies: one warm, one playful, one that moves things forward. In under 30 seconds.

✨ Bio Analyser
Paste their profile bio. Aura AI extracts the specific hooks that make great conversation starters — not generic topics like "travel", but specific talkable details. Then generates 3 personalised openers that reference those hooks.

✍️ Profile Optimiser
Paste your current bio. Aura AI rewrites it to be more compelling, authentic to your voice, and optimised for the platform — Hinge, Tinder, Bumble, or Feeld.

⚡ Pickup Lines
Generate 5 original, personalised pickup lines in your style. Choose Funny, Smooth, or Bold. No templates — every line is generated fresh.

**Why Aura AI is different**
→ Personalisation first — every output shaped by your tone profile
→ Context-aware — reads actual conversations before generating
→ Never sounds like AI — your style, your voice, your words
→ $4.99/month Pro vs $9.99–14.99 at competitors
